Product

Four layers of trading intelligence

Each layer builds on the one before it. Use the information directly, or let the AI agent on top do the analysis for you.

1

Site-specific forecasting

Numerical weather models combined with wind-farm flow simulations and machine learning calibrated on your plant data. Captures wake effects, terrain influence, and local patterns. Runs continuously with fast reforecasting as new NWP runs arrive.

Interpretable by design — a junior trader and a senior trader can both read the forecast drivers at their level.

2

Market intelligence

Real information from the energy market that a trader would otherwise gather manually from multiple sources: REMIT alerts and outage notifications, gas plant and grid maintenance schedules, demand patterns and seasonal deviations, imbalance price signals, intraday volatility and liquidity dynamics.

Not abstract “market context” — concrete, actionable market data feeds integrated into one view.

3

Risk analysis

Probabilistic uncertainty quantification, ramp risk scoring, tail risk estimates, and imbalance exposure calculations. Models run in real time to produce insights that are computationally hard to generate — this is not a simple dashboard overlay.

Presented readably for users of different expertise levels. You can always understand why the risk level is what it is.

4

AI agent

Sits on top of all three layers. The agent processes forecast uncertainty, market intelligence, and risk analysis together — and synthesises it into position recommendations tuned to your risk tolerance. It alerts you during critical moments: ramp events, model divergence, price spikes, gate closure deadlines.

For when you don't have time to analyse everything yourself. The agent does the heavy lifting; you decide whether to follow, adjust, or override.
