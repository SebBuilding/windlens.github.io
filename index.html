Under the hood

From forecast to market action

WindLens builds on two layers: a forecasting engine that captures your site's specific physics, and an AI agent that turns those forecasts into actionable trading intelligence.

Site-specific forecasting

Numerical weather models combined with wind-farm flow simulations and ML calibration. Captures wake effects, terrain influence, and local patterns. Runs continuously with fast reforecasting as new data arrives.

Market risk monitoring

Tracks imbalance prices, intraday market dynamics, and settlement patterns. Identifies when price risk is elevated and explains the drivers — not just that something changed, but why.

Risk alerts with context

When forecast uncertainty or market conditions cross critical thresholds, WindLens alerts you. Each alert explains the drivers — which weather model diverges, what historical patterns suggest — and recommends a position.
